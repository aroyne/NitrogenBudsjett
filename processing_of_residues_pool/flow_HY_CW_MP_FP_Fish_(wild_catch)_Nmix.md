# Wild fish catch

![HY.CW-MP.FP-Fish (wild catch)-Nmix](../output_files/plots/HY_CW_MP_FP_Fish_(wild_catch)_Nmix.png)

### Flow Description
found using data from Fiskeridirektoratet (2025) on total wild fish catch. According to Schäppi et al. (2025), p254: N content in fish and shellfish: 2.8% according to UNECE Guidance, Annex 6 Table 12. Our results are very close to those of Hohmann-Marriott (2025) (also when looking at shellfish and aquaculture).
