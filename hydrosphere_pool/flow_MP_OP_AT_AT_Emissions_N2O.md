# Industrial Emissions (N2O)

![MP.OP-AT.AT-Emissions-N2O](../output_files/plots/MP_OP_AT_AT_Emissions_N2O.png)

### Flow Description
**MP.OP-AT.AT-Emissions-N2O** are taken from UNFCCC common reporting tables, Table 3 as advised by Schäppi et al. (2025). Emissions are substantial, at least before 2009, and the main source of emissions is from nitric acid production.
