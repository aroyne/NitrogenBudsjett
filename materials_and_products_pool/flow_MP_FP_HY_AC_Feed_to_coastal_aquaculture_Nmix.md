# Feed to Coastal Aquaculture

![MP.FP-HY.AC-Feed to coastal aquaculture-Nmix](../output_files/plots/MP_FP_HY_AC_Feed_to_coastal_aquaculture_Nmix.png)

### Flow Description
**MP.FP-HY.AC-Feed to coastal aquaculture-Nmix**: the amount of feed per ton of produced fish is found by assuming an average protein (N) retention of 35.37 % based on values from \aas_aquaculture_2022 (n.d.). The amount of produced fish is found by using data from Fiskeridirektoratet \(fiskeridirektoratet_statistikk_2025) on sold farmed fish.\n\n\Hohmann-Marriott (2025) found the nitrogen content in aquaculture feed in 2020 to be 124 ktN, which is very similar to our results.
