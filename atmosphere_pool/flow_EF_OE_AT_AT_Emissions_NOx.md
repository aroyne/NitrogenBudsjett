# Other energy emissions (NOx)

![EF.OE-AT.AT-Emissions-NOx](../output_files/plots/EF_OE_AT_AT_Emissions_NOx.png)

### Flow Description
EF.OE-AT.AT-Emissions-NOx is NOx emissions from fuel combustion in residential, commercial and other sectors that are not already covered. We have used data from CLRTAP Inventory Submissions emep_official_2025 (n.d.)} as advised by Schäppi et al. (2025), using the categories given in Table 14.
