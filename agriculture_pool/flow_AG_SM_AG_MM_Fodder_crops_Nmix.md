# Fodder Crops Production

![AG.SM-AG.MM-Fodder crops-Nmix](../output_files/plots/AG_SM_AG_MM_Fodder_crops_Nmix.png)

### Flow Description
We have used data for grass and fodder production from SSB table 13648 «Avling i jordbruket (1000 tonn) og avling per dekar (kg), etter ymse jordbruksvekstar (F) 2021 – 2024» and 05772 «Avling i jordbruket, etter ymse jordbruksvekstar (1 000 tonn) (F) (avslutta serie) 2000 – 2020». Values prior to 2000 are found in the SSB Jordbruksstatistikk (Table 2.1/Table 20). The protein content of grass and fodder is known to be highly variable. We have assumed a protein content of 15 % based on 2025 analyses of 13 000 grass samples from all over Norway by Tine/NorFor, and 15 % N in protein (FAO, 2003). 

Hohmann-Marriott (2025) used similar data sources but arrived at a smaller N flow (40-45 ktN) using a protein content of 8 % and N content in protein of 15 % (Table S2).
