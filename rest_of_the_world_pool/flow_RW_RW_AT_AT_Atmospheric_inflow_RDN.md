# Atmospheric Inflow (Reduced N)

![RW.RW-AT.AT-Atmospheric inflow-RDN](../output_files/plots/RW_RW_AT_AT_Atmospheric_inflow_RDN.png)

### Flow Description
Is found from source-receptor data from EMEP. Long-term trends in global atmospheric inputs, specifically the balance and transport of reduced reactive nitrogen forms (such as ammonia emissions from land), are evaluated by \\citet{fowler_global_2013} and \\citet{ackerman_global_2019}. There is a change in methodology in the EMEP reporting between 2002 and 2003 data.
