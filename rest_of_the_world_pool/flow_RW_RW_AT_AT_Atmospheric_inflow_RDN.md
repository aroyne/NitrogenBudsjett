# Atmospheric Inflow (Reduced N)

![RW.RW-AT.AT-Atmospheric inflow-RDN](../output_files/plots/RW_RW_AT_AT_Atmospheric_inflow_RDN.png)

### Flow Description
Is found from source-receptor data from EMEP. Long-term trends in global atmospheric inputs, specifically the balance and transport of reduced reactive nitrogen forms (such as ammonia emissions from land), are evaluated by Fowler (2013) and Ackerman (2019). There is a change in methodology in the EMEP reporting between 2002 and 2003 data.
