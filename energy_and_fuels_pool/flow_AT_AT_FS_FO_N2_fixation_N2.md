# Biological N2 Fixation (Forest)

![AT.AT-FS.FO-N2 fixation-N2](../output_files/plots/AT_AT_FS_FO_N2_fixation_N2.png)

### Flow Description
Following the Swedish NBB Moldan et al. (2025), we use an N-fixation rate of 1.5 kg/ha/year and a forested area of 12.0 mill ha as given by SSB for 2019-2023 (table 14368); we assume this value is constant for our entire time period. This gives an annual N-fixation rate of 18.0 ktN. For comparison, the value for Sweden in 2015 was found to be 39.5 ktN Moldan et al. (2025).

### References

* Moldan, F., Stadmark, J., Jutterström, S., & Ljunggren, J. (2025). Where does Sweden’s nitrogen go? Building a comprehensive national nitrogen budget. *Environmental Research Letters, 20*(12), 124068. https://doi.org/10.1088/1748-9326/ae2697
